Director (Singapore)

My work experience started while doing my ACA in Singapore immediately on completion of my National Service in 1981. 1983 – 1999: Started and established an export business in Singapore, catering to the requirement of the budding computer industry in India. The products included computer peripherals, electronic and electrical components, software and other raw materials for the computer , instrumentation and electronic industry. 2000 – 2017: Diversified into Infra Structure Development and representing Singapore and Malaysian companies for Projects in India.

In 2000, I led the team in the tender enrolment and participation process for the construction of the Tidel Park in Taramani, Chennai for Econ Piling (Singapore). We achieved L2. In the same year I represented Asbil (Malaysia) and tendered for the de-silting of 4 major rivers and waterways in around Chennai under the supervision of the PWD. We achieved L1 for all 4 tenders but were awarded the contract for Adyar River only. From 2001, I have been active in the promotion of Inter-Linking of River Waters Programme along with NAWAD TECH / Madurai. My role includes presentation of the concept and discussions with the Ministers and Government Officials and more recently, general public! I also handle foreign meetings and working out JV participation with NAWAD and other MNCs. Was instrumental in proposing a solution to Bihar State in 2012 to alleviate their flood situation and it has been accepted by the State and very recently by the Centre, on similar lines. I am presently taking up the cause with NITI Aayog, AP & TS Governments I also evaluate and help purchase/lease real estate for corporates, especially those coming from Malaysia, Singapore and UK. Marketing of residential properties to NRI potential customers is also handled.

In the area of technology, I am marketing solar installations for both Karnataka and Sri Lanka and have been manufacturing & marketing LED street lights and lighting systems from Tescom (Bangalore Electronic City 2) where I had helped set-up the mainstay EMS services in 2000. Under the Micro Tech Global Foundation (MTGF Mumbai) banner, I have also been recommending what the Government could consider as appropriate for Smart City Development. Besides suggesting our Waterways and its potential to generate huge amount of hydro power and clean navigation systems, we have suggested Smart Grid Energy Metering with the development of Firmware for the same, dual city concepts, retail parks and bio-mass plants amongst others.
